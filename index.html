Online

You can register online 24 hours a day at the Washington Secretary of State's website. You will need to register online at least 8 days in advance of the next election.

  • A current Washington State driver license, or
  • A current Washington State ID card, or
  • Last 4 digits of Social Security Number

By mail

FAQs

What is the order of candidates in the local voters' pamphlet?
Candidates in the local voters' pamphlet appear in the order they will appear on the ballot.

Why are there candidates and measures in the voters' pamphlets that are not in the ballot?
The candidates and measures on your ballot reflect your registered district.

How are committees in favor or in opposition to a measure formed?
Districts appoint committee members who agree to write statements.
